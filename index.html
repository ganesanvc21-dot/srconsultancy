Who We Are
A Legacy Since 1995

SR Consultancy began its journey in 1995 as the Arab Emirates Training Centre in Virudhachalam, Tamil Nadu. For over three decades, we have been the most reliable and affordable partner for manpower solutions across the Gulf region.

Today, we operate from two offices — our founding base in Virudhachalam and our UAE office in Mussafah, Abu Dhabi — giving us deep roots in both the source and destination markets.
